About Our App

EBud is an application that instantly connects the School and the Parents under one platform. It has a very simple and Easy to use interface for everyone. Here are some features of the App.

Attendance

Instant notification and detailed view of the student attendance in the current academic year including presents, absentees and leaves. Parents can leave a note with the reason of absenteeism.

Announcements

Instant alerts on any circular/notice from school and also complete archieve thereof with option of attachment download.

Online Fee Payment

Parents can view the complete fee information including fee payable, payment status and dues for different fee types.

Bus Track

Get real time updates of the Buses, easy for schools and parents to monitor the route and can for keepig track of their student/child.

Results

As soon as results are declared at school, parents can view and download the progress report card on their mobile.

Syllabus & Homeworks

Instant updates about the Homeworks and Syllabus, parents can keep track of the portions covered by the school and can view the homeworks assigned for a particular day and syllabus as a whole for an academic session.
